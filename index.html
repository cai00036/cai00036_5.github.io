Wei is a Multimedia Designer based in Ottawa. She loves to create short-form video content that engages young people. Wei is not only a creative thinker and innovator, she also brings a unique perspective to her team. Coming from China, she offers an invaluable creative and problem-solving lens. During her studies in in Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College, Wei discovered her passion video and design. Now, she specializes in motion graphics and animation for CMYKids. When she is not in the throes of video production, Wei spends her time learning English and master the violin.
